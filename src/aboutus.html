Who are we?

TECHWARTS is the technical community of specialized technical communities at PES University Electronic City Campus which includes four field/community as of now that are as follows
Pixel - Augmented Reality And Virtual Reality
Predict This! - Machine Learning and Artificial intelligence
We the Programmers - Competitive Coding
Oncreate() - Mobile Computing and Android

Our Vision

Imagine a world where every person finds like minded people to work with and learn and build innovative projects and enhance his or her skills. TECHWARTS was started with the vision of forming a technical community of people with like-min ,in order to find a platform where we can build interest oriented skills through learn and let learn basically peer to peer learning and collaborations

More about us

With the guidance and mentorship of Dr Sandesh Jayanth (Head of Department, Dept of CSE at PESU-ECC ) we started our journey on 12th October,2018(Friday). From that day till now we have come a long way in establishing a regularly functioning club where enthusiastic students have always taken part and been with the community .We looked at the current trending Tec buzzing skills and carefully choose four communities /clubs in Techwarts .
