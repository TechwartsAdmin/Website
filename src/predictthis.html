Who are we?

We are the machine learning and Deep learning club at PESU-ECC. Since the vary beggning of Techwarts we have organised countless sessions and several workshops on machine learning, deep learn and their implementations in the real world.

Our Vision

Automation is eating up jobs. From managing over 40% of the New York Stake exchange to driving you to work, it's not naive to say that in 10 years we'll be out of work if we don't reinvent ourselves. Our vision is to help everyone prepare for the future. With us, master AI before it masters you.
